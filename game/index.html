About the Game

The Story

Join our brave little mouse on an epic escape adventure! Trapped in a house full of cunning enemies, you'll need all your wit and courage to navigate through dangerous rooms, solve puzzles, and ultimately find freedom. Each step could be your last - can you outsmart the enemies and make The Grand Mouse Escape?

Key Features

3 Unique Biomes

Explore the cozy House, mysterious Forest, and dangerous Dungeon environments

Challenging Battles

Face off against different enemies with unique attack patterns and strategies

Cozy Pixel Art

Beautiful pixel art with warm, inviting colors and charming animations

Collectible Items

Gather cheese, apples, and other treats while avoiding deadly traps

Characters

The Mouse: Our brave protagonist with quick reflexes and a clever mind.
The Enemies: Various foes with different abilities and hunting patterns.
And more characters to discover...
